# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup founders, pre-seed to Series B. They arrive from a LinkedIn share, a referral, or a YC community post. They're evaluating a motion design partner for a specific near-term moment: a launch, a fundraise deck, a product demo. They are busy, skeptical of vendors, and make fast decisions. They want to see the work immediately and know whether Nox understands their world.

## Product Purpose

Nox makes motion design for tech teams: product explainers, walkthroughs, and launch videos built around specific founder moments. The site's one job is to convert a visiting founder into a concept call. Everything — the work, the copy, the pricing — exists to reduce friction toward that booking.

## Brand Personality

Sharp and direct. Gets to the point before the founder has time to wonder why they should care. No padding, no vendor-speak, no aspirational filler. Treats the visitor like they're intelligent and in a hurry. Three words: precise, unadorned, credible.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: white/light backgrounds, hero-metric templates (big number + small label), floating device mockups, feature-icon grids, testimonial carousels.
- Crypto/web3 dark: neon on black, glow effects, particle systems, digital-noise textures used decoratively, gradient text.

## Design Principles

1. **The work is the argument.** Every design decision should make the videos easier to see and harder to ignore. UI chrome that competes with the work is wrong.
2. **Earn every word.** Copy that restates what the visual already says gets cut. Founders read at scan speed.
3. **Craft signals craft.** The precision of the UI — spacing, alignment, typographic tension — is itself a proof point. Sloppiness in the shell reads as sloppiness in the deliverable.
4. **Direct lines to the call.** Section transitions and copy arcs should always end with a reason to book. The CTA is not decoration.
5. **Dark because the work demands it.** The dark theme exists so videos render at full luminance against a near-black surface, not as a stylistic default.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Keyboard-navigable interactive elements. Sufficient contrast for body text and UI labels. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for all scroll-triggered and entrance animations (already implemented in anime.js hooks).
