Building the future of electromagnetic launch

At Auriga, innovation starts in the lab but lives in the field.

We’re a team of engineers, innovators, and builders rethinking how motion is created. With backgrounds in aerospace, defense, and advanced manufacturing, our team develops scalable, reusable launch systems powered entirely by electricity, and not chemical propellants.

Our technology bridges precision engineering with sustainable power. By replacing propellants with electromagnetism, we eliminate the waste, complexity, and cost of conventional propulsion systems. Every test, every prototype, and every launcher we build moves us closer to proving that clean systems can outperform traditional ones, not just in theory, but in measurable performance.

From our vertically-integrated R&D facility in Orange County, CA, we design, build, and validate every component in-house. This hands-on approach lets us move fast, iterate quickly, and push beyond the limits of conventional aerospace and defense development andmanufacturing.

We’re not just building a new kind of launcher: we’re building the foundation for a cleaner, more effective and more reusable future in defense and space.
